## The Concept

**Escape Alt Control** is a four-track minimal techno album that traces the journey from passive algorithmic consumption to conscious disconnection. It's a concept album for the algorithmic age — sonifying the feed, then tearing it down.

The album explores surveillance capitalism and the attention economy through a four-act narrative:

1. **Seduction** — The feed feels harmless, even pleasant
2. **Entrapment** — The pace accelerates, anxiety builds
3. **Rupture** — Conscious rejection, violent break
4. **Aftermath** — Uncertain freedom, quiet isolation

Each track represents a stage in breaking free from feed-driven chaos. No General MIDI dependencies — all sounds are synthesized or sampled from a validated palette, ensuring reproducible timbral identity across environments.

## Track Listing

### 01. Algorithmic Drift
**BPM:** 130 | **Key:** F minor | **Duration:** ~4 minutes

*The gentle seduction of the feed.*

A minimal techno opener establishing the hypnotic pull of algorithmic curation. A steady 4/4 kick, subtle percussion, and drifting synth patterns slowly evolve — mirroring how we slip into endless scrolling without noticing.

**Production:**
- Clean 4/4 kick pattern
- Subtle hi-hat textures
- Wandering bassline
- Sparse atmospheric pads
- Gradual evolution throughout

**Mood:** Hypnotic, seductive, deceptively calm

---

### 02. Feed Loop
**BPM:** 145 | **Key:** C# minor | **Duration:** ~4 minutes

*Anxious 16th-note chaos.*

The tempo increases as the feed accelerates. Rapid 16th-note patterns, layered percussion, and increasingly complex rhythmic structures represent the anxiety of information overload. The music becomes denser, more frantic — you're still scrolling, but now you're feeling it.

**Production:**
- Driving 16th-note hi-hats
- Layered clap/snare patterns
- Aggressive bass stabs
- Glitchy percussion fills
- Building tension throughout

**Mood:** Anxious, frantic, overwhelming

---

### 03. Escape Alt Control
**BPM:** 160 → 80 (half-time breakdown) | **Key:** A minor | **Duration:** ~4 minutes

*Peak rupture. The system breaks.*

The title track and album climax. Polyrhythms, glitch explosions, and violent rhythmic ruptures represent the moment of conscious rejection. Breakbeats collide with techno foundations — the sound of tearing down the machine from inside.

**Production:**
- Polyrhythmic drum patterns
- Breakbeat interruptions
- Glitch/stutter effects
- Dissonant synth bursts
- Half-time breakdown section
- Maximum chaos, then collapse

**Mood:** Violent, chaotic, liberating

---

### 04. Offline Ghost Mode
**BPM:** 90 | **Key:** D major (relative to A minor) | **Duration:** ~4 minutes

*What's left after escape.*

Sparse ambient finale. After the chaos of track 3, the dust settles. Natural sounds, long silences, gentle melodic fragments. This is the uncertainty of freedom — quiet, unfamiliar, but yours.

**Production:**
- Minimal percussion (or none)
- Ambient pad textures
- Field recordings / natural sounds
- Long decay times
- Sparse melodic motifs
- Uncertain, open-ended resolution

**Mood:** Quiet, uncertain, isolated, free
